NumCode

Number Patterns

Are humans capable of thinking of truly random numbers? I don't think so. I trained a pattern-finding statistical model ('AI') to determine if a number you entered is actually random or you just typed in random digits. Try generating "truly" random numbers using dice, Google or any other semi-random generator and trying those out. Can my model detect when you think of them vs when they're generated?

Enter a number below and click on the Analyze-button and find out what my model thinks about your number.

This works best when you're using a phone to enter the numbers, as you type differently when using a mobile device. Of course, this doesn't work every time. It probably works about 90% of the time for most people, especially when they try to enter a number quickly. If you have any questions regarding this website, feel free to email me here.
